About us

Welcome to myJanken, which translates from Japanese to my Rock-Paper-Scissors!

This game involves gesture-based "Rock, Paper, Scissors," allowing players to play against the computer by showing gestures in front of a camera.

The game uses machine learning to recognize hand gestures via a webcam and automatically classifies them.
